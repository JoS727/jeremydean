Short bio

Oakland roots. San Diego soul.

Jeremy Dean is a singer, songwriter, and producer originally from Oakland, California, now residing in San Diego. He blends R&B and reggae with soulful vocals, emotive lyrics, and a live presence that feels warm, direct, and unforgettable.

Whether it is a headline room, a local showcase, or a summer-night crowd that needs something worth staying for, Jeremy brings the kind of live energy people remember after the night ends.
